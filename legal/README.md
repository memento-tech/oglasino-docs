# Legal

Holds pre-lawyer drafts of legal documents — Privacy Policy, Terms of
Service, and similar — authored by Docs/QA from a structured intake with
Igor. These drafts are explicitly **not legal advice** and must be
reviewed and finalized by an external lawyer before publication. Files
use kebab-case slugs (e.g., `privacy-policy-draft.md`,
`terms-of-service-draft.md`).
